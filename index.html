ABOUT ME

Born in El Salvador, raised in the San Francisco Bay Area. I have always been both left- and right-brained. After human being, I consider myself an artist. I like making nice, useful, or amusing things. My brain also gets tickled by puzzles and analytics. I enjoy deep thinking and pattern finding. My current technical skills include: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Bootstrap, jQuery, APIs, JSON, AJAX, WordPress, Adobe (XD, Illustrator, Photoshop, inDesign), Google AppScript, and Vue.js.


I am always learning new things for proffesional and personal reasons. Talk to me about football, growing avocadoes, or about the last thing you've watched on Netflix.
